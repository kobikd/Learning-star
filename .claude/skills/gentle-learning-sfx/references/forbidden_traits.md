# Forbidden Traits — כוכב הלמידה

These qualities must NEVER appear in any sound for this app.
Gefen has sensory sensitivities (dyspraxia, HFA) — any of these could cause
distress, shutdown, or a negative learning association.

## Forbidden textures
- metallic, harsh, sharp, abrasive, grating, screechy, buzzy, crunchy (aggressive), gritty
- distorted, glitchy, broken, crackling, static, noisy, clipping

## Forbidden dynamics
- loud, blaring, booming, explosive, punchy, slamming, banging, crashing
- sudden, jarring, startling, shocking (dynamic surprises)

## Forbidden emotional tones
- scary, dark, creepy, eerie, ominous, threatening, tense, anxious, aggressive
- angry, frustrated, deeply sad, mournful, sinister

## Forbidden associations
- alarm, siren, buzzer, error, warning, notification, alert, ringtone
- thunder, horn, klaxon, scream

## Forbidden musical qualities
- dissonant, atonal, strongly minor-key, chromatic tension, tritone interval
- aggressive staccato, fortissimo, sforzando
- descending minor seconds or tritones (feel "wrong" to many autistic children)

## Forbidden in Hebrew voice lines
- The word "טָעִית" (you were wrong) — replace with "נְנַסֶּה יַחַד!" (let's try together)
- The word "לֹא" alone as feedback — replace with redirection
- Masculine address (Gefen is a girl — always use feminine forms: "לַחְצִי" not "לְחַץ")
- Fast speech — Gefen needs rate ≤ 0.82 for instructions, ≤ 0.70 for letters/numbers
- Robot-sounding TTS — warm, natural delivery only

## Prompt writing rules — negative phrasing
Never describe what a sound is NOT when writing ElevenLabs prompts.
The model latches onto negated concepts. Use positive descriptions only.

| Instead of... | Write... |
|---|---|
| "not scary" | "gentle and friendly" |
| "not loud" | "soft and quiet" |
| "not harsh" | "smooth and rounded" |
| "no alarm" | "calm feedback chime" |
| "not robotic" | "warm and natural" |

Exception: brief safety fences like "no harsh attack" and "no metallic tone" are
fine because ElevenLabs handles them well.
